About Us

Typically skydives are carried out from around 4,000m
(or 12,500ft) offering 40 to 50 seconds of freefall time.
Longer free fall times can be achieved by exiting an aircraft at
altitudes much higher than 4,000m, but very high jumps require
pressurized oxygen within the aircraft, and bottled oxygen for the diver.
